# Executive Dashboard Spec (Business Health)

## Goal
Single-screen executive view of growth, profitability, retention, and profit leakage.

## Top KPIs (cards)
- Net Revenue
- Orders
- AOV
- Gross Profit
- Gross Margin %
- Refund Amount
- Shipping Cost % of Revenue
- SLA Breach %
- Active Customers
- Churned Customers (if imported as a metric table)

## Visuals
- Revenue & Orders trend (dual-axis line)
- Gross Profit by Category (bar)
- Discount % by Category (bar)
- Profit Leakage Waterfall: Discounts → Shipping → Refunds
- Map: Net Revenue by Region/Country

## Slicers
- Date
- Region/Country/City
- Category
- Channel
- Payment method
